ABOUT THE EVENT

TELEMOR CONNEXT 2019 is the technology exhibition and 7th anniversary of Telemor in Timor-Leste. Continue the success of Telemor Go Digital 2018, Telemor Connext 2019 with be a golden opportunities for government officers, company and organization leaders to meet, discuss and experience applicable modern technologies for a digitalized Timor-Leste.

CONNEXT is the combination of "Connection" and "Next" which literally means that connection for the next era.

  • CONNECTING EXPERTS: To share knowledge of modern technology and digitalization
  • CONNECTING PARTNERS: To strengthen partnership and foster promising projects for digitalizing Timor-Leste.
  • CONNECTING MODERN TECHNOLOGY: For better user experience, and for easier and more convenient life in Timor-Leste
