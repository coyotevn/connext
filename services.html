MOSAN - FIRST STEP TOWARDS E-COMMERCE AND CASHLESS FUTURE IN TIMOR-LESTE


Mr. Abrão de Vasconcelos - Governor of the Bank Central of Timor-Leste registered to be customer of Mosan service.

Licensed in 8/2018 and officially launched in 10/2018, Mosan service (Money transfer and e-wallet) by Telemor Fintech is now the only e-wallet in Timor-Leste. Mosan allows customers to transfer money instantly, make mobile top-up, mobile payment easily anytime, anywhere by just using their mobile phone.

In the meantime, Telemor Fintech will cooperate with its partners to introduce more new and useful services, such as: Mosan mobile application, Electricity top-up, School tuition payment, QR-code payment in retailers, Virtual supermarket . to make life more convenient than ever for its users in Timor-Leste.

In financial inclusion report by Bank Central of Timor-Leste, the launching of Mosan is considered to be main factor to one among 4 key development of Timor-Leste finance industry during the period of 2016 - 2018. Mosan is believed to be the very first step of Telemor towards the e-commerce and cashless future in Timor-Leste.
