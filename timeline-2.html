Telemor - The journey to a better Timor-Leste

2012: INCREDIBLY FAST NETWORK ESTABLISHMENT

On 31/7/2012 Viettel win the bidding and receive license for investing in Timor-Leste. Less than 1 month later, on 22/08/2012, Viettel Timor-Leste received business operation license in Timor-Leste and become the second Telecom provider in Timor-Leste.

On 6/9/2012 the team of the first 13 Vietnamese experts landed in Timor-Leste, opening up months of Vietnamese and Timorese colleagues' working day and night to construct network infrastructure over 13 municipalities in Timor-Leste. The setup team successfully overcome serious difficulties of rainy season, mountainous topographic of Timor-Leste as to quickly setup nationwide telecom network infrastructure and its distribution channels within an incredible amount of time.


2013: LAUNCHING TELECOM SERVICE UNDER THE BRAND OF TELEMOR

On the date of 08/02/2013, the First Call had been set up and connected to Vietnam for the President of Viettel Group successfully. The switchboard was installed & set up 100% only by staff of Viettel & Telemor.

On 22/05/2013, Viettel Timor-Leste officially got the brand name of "Telemor", which is a combination of the word "telecommunications" and "more" − implying a telecom operator that always try to develop and provide Timor-Leste people with creative services and the best benefit. "Telemor" is pronounced similarly to the word "Timor" and "Amor" − which means "love" in local Tetun language. This creates a close relationship between the brand name and Timor-Leste country.

By 10/7/2013, Telemor is ready for the service with 97 BTS along with back-bone optical fiber. At the same time, the company started spreading the business to all 13 districts of Timor Leste in the anticipation of Timorese people. After 06 months running the business, Telemor reached the number of 200,000 subscribers, all the other targets were reached and even surpassed.

2014: WORLDWIDE RECOGNIZED

START UP OF THE YEAR

10/10/2014 Telemor got the Silver Prize of Stevie Awards for "Start-up of the Year". By the time receiving the Award, Telemor had reached 300,000 subscribers.

After 1 year of starting its business activities in Timor-Leste, Telemor ranked 1st in terms of network infrastructure and sales distribution channels with 2500 points of sales, 160 2G telecom tower, 160 3G tower, 1600km of fiber optic cable covering 96% population of Timor-Leste.

By the end of 2014, Telemor reached 420.000 mobile subscribers, outnumbered subscribers that the biggest telecom operator before have ever reached after more than 10 years of operating in Timor-Leste. The rate of voice call was reported to have decreased 18 times in comparison with the previously monopoly period. Telecom services, from then, were no longer a luxurious services only for rich customers.

2015: BEST OPERATOR IN EMERGING MARKET

On 23/10/2015 Telemor received the Gold Prize of Stevie Awards for "Fastest Growing Company of the Year in Asia, Australia and New Zealand". And by this time, Telemor already reached a remarkable milestone of 500,000 subscribers, accounting for 51% market share of telecommunication industry in Timor-Leste.

The streak continued when on 20/11/2015, Telemor was honored with the Award of "Best Operator in an Emerging Market" from the World Communications Awards (WCA) 2015.

2016: NOT ONLY A TELECOM OPERATOR BUT ALSO TECHNOLOGY COMPANY

The year of 2016 marks a turning point of Telemor from a mere telecommunication company to a telecom and ICT services providers.

In addition to the current 3G Internet service, Telemor started providing web-based entertainment service to Timorese customers such as: Muzika.tl, Klip.tl,... to meet the thirst for entertainment content in Timor-Leste market. We was also the first to shake hands with worldwide technology giant to install Google server inside Telemor's data center in Timor-Leste. This cooperation helps significantly increase the Internet access speed to Google's services such as Youtube, Google search, Google Drive for Telemor Internet users in Timor-Leste. By the end of 2016, Telemor has reached the magnificent number of 555,000 subscribers and held the position of No.1 Network Operator in Timor-Leste.

Besides serving individual mobile customers, Telemor took its first step in providing infrastructure and ICT solution to government, enterprise and organizations. In 2016, Telemor won the bid to become the contractor for national connectivity project (NCPVI). The project helps facilitate connection infrastructure for 140 government agencies all over Timor-Leste with 2000km fiber optic cables in all districts. NCPVI created a promising basis for Timor-Leste's future e-government and also opened up potential opportunities for other Telemor's projects at national level.

2017: THE FIRST TO LAUNCH NATIONWIDE 4G NETWORK IN TIMOR-LESTE

On July 2017, Telemor was the first telecom operator in Timor-Leste to official launch its nationwide 4G network in Timor-Leste. At the time of launching, Telelmor's 4G network covered all 13/13 districts which make it possible for 70% of Timor-Leste population to access 4G technology high-speed Internet connection. Until now, Telemor's 4G network still hold the 1st place in terms of network coverage with signal available to 98% of population in Timor-Leste.

In the same year, Cinema.tl by Telemor became the most popular ever Tetum/Bahasa Indonesia subtitled film website in Timor-Leste with more than 30.000 active users. The roll-out of Cinema.tl provides Timorense customers with a new way of enjoy the most updated world-class blockbuster movies at a cheaper price than ever.

By 2017, Telemor's enterprise solution unit marked its milestone by winning the project of implementing Data Center for Timor-Leste prime minister office and also establishing long-term partnership with BCNTL, PNTL, ANPM and many other big government agencies, organizations and companies.

2018: GO DIGITAL

Despite complicated political situation in Timor-Leste, Telemor kept growing and introducing new services to its value customers.

THE MOST POPULAR MOBILE TV APPLICATION IN TIMOR-LESTE

On 15/6/2018, the launching of Timor TV - the first mobile TV application in Timor-Leste was warmly adopted by Timorese users. Timor TV was an ultimate solutions for Timor-Leste people to enjoy World Cup only on their smart devices with Telemor's Internet connection. The application quickly gained its first 10.000 users in the first week of launching by making it possible for even low-income customers to join the energetic atmosphere of the planet's hottest football championship. Until 15/6/2019, after one year of being released, Timor TV reached more than 50.000 active users and successfully changed TV watching habit of a large part of mobile users in Timor-Leste.

2 years after successfully establishing partnership with Google in 2016, in June 2018, Telemor continued to shake hands with the world's biggest and most popular social media − Facebook to install Facebook server in Telemor's Data Center in Timor-Leste. The installation brought about faster access speed to Facebook for all Telemor users over the country and significantly improve social media social experience.

THE BEGINNING OF MOBILE FINANCIAL SERVICE

In August 2019, Telemor received license from Bank Central of Timor-Leste to provide e-wallet (mobile money) service in Timor-Leste. The service of "Mosan" (literally means Mobile money in Tetun) is publicly launched in October 2018, allowing customers to send, receive money and do mobile top-up only on their phone. Mosan agents quickly spread to all 13/13 districts and outnumbered the access points of any banks in the country. The launching of Mosan revolutionized the way Timorese transfer money and make it accessible for people in non-central area to access mobile financial services. In BCTL's report on financial inclusion, Mosan service is identified as main contributor to one of the four key developments of Timor-Leste's banking industry over the period of 2016 − 2018.

2019: CONNECTION FOR THE FUTURE

NETWORK UPGRADE AND BRAND IMAGE LEVERAGING

During the first half of 2019, the telecom operator from Vietnam deployed many communications and advertising activities as to rebrand itself with a youthful, dynamic and professional image in all communications channels, following customer-centric philosophy.

In March 2019, Telemor implemented its national network upgrade that promise to dramatically broaden its 4G network coverage. In addition, the company also optimize and rearrange its network resource to bring more privilege to our VIP customers.

EVERYTHING CONNECTED IN TIMOR GALAXY APPLICATION

In August 2019, Telemor officially launched its all-in-one application with a view to connect all applications and digital services into one. The application named Timor Galaxy allows it users to make video call to each other with better video quality thanks to using Telemor's widest network of fiber optic cable around the country. Timor Galaxy's app users can also check and manage their own mobile services, stay tuned with most updated news from Hallo.tl, hottest music playlists from Muzika.tl, most blockbuster movies from Cinema.tl and never miss their favorite TV programs from Timor TV.

BOOMING YEAR OF MOSAN SERVICE

2019 is also a flourishing year of Mosan service with 320 newly developed agents over the country. Mosan users grew significantly at a monthly rate of 40%. In May 2019, Mosan has launched its mobile application and now planned to trial and launch many new useful functions such as: QR code payment, Electricity top-up payment, Salary payment and school fee payment. Mosan is believed to be taking its first steps towards the future of e-commerce and cashless society in Timor-Leste.
